Red Velvet

Red Velvet is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The group is composed of five members: Irene, Seulgi, Wendy, Joy, and Yeri. Red Velvet debuted on August 1, 2014, with their single "Ice Cream Cake".The group is known for their dual concept, which alternates between their "red" and "velvet" sides. The "red" side is characterized by bright, energetic concepts, while the "velvet" side is characterized by more mature and sophisticated concepts.

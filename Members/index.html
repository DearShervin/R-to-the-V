R to the V

Irene

Irene (아이린) is the leader, main rapper, and lead dancer of the group. She is also the oldest member, born on March 29, 1991 in Daegu, South Korea. She is known for her elegant and charismatic stage presence, as well as her sweet and caring personality off-stage. She likes to read books and watch movies in her spare time.

Seulgi

Seulgi (슬기) is the main dancer and lead vocalist of the group. She is also the second oldest member, She was born on March 10, 1994 in Ansan, South Korea. She is known for her powerful and versatile vocals, as well as her amazing dancing skills. She is also very artistic and creative, and enjoys drawing and painting.

Joy

Joy (조이) is the lead rapper and sub-vocalist of the group. She is also the second youngest member, born in 1996. She is known for her bright and energetic stage presence, as well as her witty and playful humor off-stage. Joy is also a talented actress, having starred in several dramas and films.

Wendy

Wendy (웬디) is the main vocalist and lead dancer of the group. She is also the third oldest member, born in 1994. She is known for her soulful and expressive voice, as well as her cheerful and bubbly personality and she if one the best vocalists in the whole industry. She is fluent in English and Korean, and can also speak some French and Spanish.

Yeri

Yeri (예리) is the maknae (youngest member), sub-vocalist, and sub-rapper of the group. She joined the group in 2015, making them a five-member group. She is known for her cute and charming image, as well as her mature and confident attitude. She likes to write songs and poems in her spare time.
